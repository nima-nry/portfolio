## Objective
Forklift-mounted telescopic jib for **EV battery handling** in Gigafactory assembly lines.

## Key Features
- **Load Capacity**: 1.5 tons  
- **Extension**: 1 m → 2.5 m  
- **Fabrication**: SMAW/GMAW welding (real photos below)

## Design & Fabrication
- **CAD**: SOLIDWORKS 2024  
- **Renders**: High-quality visualization (**X renders available**) <!-- X = تعداد عکسهات -->
- **Built & Tested**: Real load test up to 1.6 tons
